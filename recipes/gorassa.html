Gorassa

Description

A light, fluffy and absorbant pancake served alongside many sauce based dishes and stews

Ingredients

  • 500g wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 500ml water
  • 1tsp salt

Steps

  1. Sift flour into a bowl.
  2. Add baking powder and salt and stir in water until batter is thick.
  3. Pour ladle-full of batter onto non-stick fat frying pan and flatten out until evenly spread.
  4. Fry at medium heat and flip when golden on one side.
