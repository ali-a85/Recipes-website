Tamia

Description

Sudanese version of deep fried falafel made from chickpeas, herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • 500g chickpeas
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander
  • 1 white bread roll
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seed
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Soak chickpeas in water for at least 3 hours.
  2. Take a skillet and cook chickpeas with minced garlic, onion and fresh coriander.
  3. Now soak broken up bread roll in water. Then break them up finely.
  4. Add flour to bread and mix.
  5. Add it into first mixture. Stir in salt, pepper, coriander seeds and set aside for 15 minutes. Add baking powder and mix well.
  6. Shape the batter into small bowls and sprinkle sesame seeds.
  7. Take a frying pan and deep fry the balls in oil.
